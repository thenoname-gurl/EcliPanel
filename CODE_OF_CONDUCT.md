# EclipseSystems Community Code of Conduct
Version 1.0 - 2026  
A project of Misiu LLC  
Authorized steward: Maksym Huzun (noname@eclipsesystems.org)

This project is built on respect, clarity, and professionalism. To maintain a
safe and productive environment for all contributors and users, the following
rules apply to all interactions within EclipseSystems repositories, issue
trackers, discussions, and communication channels.

## 1. Expected Behavior
All participants are expected to:
- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Provide clear, actionable feedback when reporting issues or suggesting
  improvements.
- Assume good intentions and engage in good-faith discussion.
- Follow all project licenses, policies, and security guidelines.
- Respect the decisions of maintainers and authorized stewards.

## 2. Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors are prohibited:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Posting or distributing malicious code, exploits, or harmful content.
- Attempting to bypass license restrictions or attribution requirements.
- Misrepresenting affiliation with EclipseSystems or Misiu LLC.
- Using project resources for commercial purposes without authorization.
- Publishing security vulnerabilities without following the responsible
  disclosure process described in SECURITY.md.

## 3. Enforcement
Violations may result in:
- Temporary or permanent bans from participation.
- Rejection of contributions.
- Reporting of malicious activity to relevant platforms or authorities.

Enforcement decisions are made exclusively by EclipseSystems under Misiu LLC
and authorized stewards designated by Maksym Huzun.

## 5. Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All EclipseSystems repositories
- All forks and derivative works created under the Community License
- All interactions in project-managed spaces

## 6. Amendments
This document may be updated by EclipseSystems under Misiu LLC or by
authorized stewards as needed to maintain a safe and productive community.
